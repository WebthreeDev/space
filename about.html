METAVERSE

DEEPSPACE is a leading metaverse crypto game on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) blockchain. It features a 3D space-based metaverse where players can trade and upgrade spaceships, explore, mine for resources and battle AI opponents and other players. The project brings multiple proprietary innovations to the crypto gaming industry.

Metaverse

Dare to explore the DEEPSPACE metaverse?

Fancy yourself as a rogue starship captain keen to make your fortune mining on alien worlds? Or a battle hardened combat veteran or mercenary ready to battle others amongst the stars in turn based strategy combat, where skill and cunning are required to survive the day. Maybe you want to try to make your fortune as a ship trader, or bring others together and ascend to the top of a guild or faction. All these paths and more can be explored in the DEEPSPACE metaverse. What will your path to thrills, adventure and riches be?

DEVELOPMENT

DEEPSPACE leverages blockchain, Play-to-Earn (P2E) and Non-Fungible Token (NFT) technologies, and brings them together in our innovative 3D space-based metaverse. The project brings multiple proprietary innovations to the crypto gaming industry.

Innovation

WHAT MAKES DEEPSPACE SPECIAL?

Utilizing proprietary technology and innovations, the team spent months pre-development getting DEEPSPACE's foundation’s right: game mechanics, infrastructure, concept designs, metaverse lore, smart contracts, tokenomics and more!

That strong foundation enables quality on-time project deliverables. The passion of our team and community is unrivaled! We're a metaverse game that puts great gameplay first. Our talented team are all passionate and experienced gamers.

Our vision is DEEPSPACE (DPS) being one of the top gaming projects in crypto. Everyone on our team and everyone in our community will do everything in their power to get us there.
