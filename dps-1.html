The Hangar

Picking your best fleet is a strategy in itself!

What’s the best fleet to play with? You can play with a maximum of four ships at a time in-game. Most players prefer having a rounded fleet with one of each ship class (tank, miner, support, fighter) in their Hangar. There are other strategies that can be leveraged for profit such as stacking your Hangar with two or more fighters or tanks.

ECOSYSTEM SECURITY

BUG BOUNTY PROGRAM

Bug bounty programs are open invitations to security researchers to discover and disclose potential vulnerabilities in a project’s smart contracts and applications, thereby protecting the project and its users. For their good work, security researchers receive a reward based on the severity of the vulnerability uncovered.

Orbitrium offers bug bounty rewards of $50,000 for Critical level vulnerabilities, $30,000 for High level vulnerabilities and $5,000 for Medium level vulnerabilities.

Orbitrium's bug bounty program is managed by Immunefi, one of the premier bug bounty and smart contract security firms.
